Occupational Risk Prevention Document

1. Introduction

The objective of this document is to establish occupational risk prevention measures at GillGalaxy, ensuring a safe and healthy environment for all workers. This document is based on current regulations on occupational risk prevention in Spain.

2. Risk Identification

Physical Risks, Falls and slips: Wet, oily or messy surfaces. Cuts and burns: Use of knives, kitchen machinery and hot surfaces. Ergonomic risks: Incorrect postures and lifting heavy loads.
Cleaning products: Handling of cleaning chemicals.
Food Handling: Cross contamination and improper handling of perishable foods.

3. Prevention Measures

Training and training: Provide continuous training to employees on occupational health and safety.
Personal protective equipment (PPE): Provide and require the use of gloves, aprons, non-slip footwear, etc.
Adequate signage: Place warning signs in risk areas.
Falls and Slips, Cuts and Burns, Ergonomic Risks and Cleaning Products

4. Emergency Procedures

First aid: Have an accessible first aid kit and staff trained in its use.
Evacuation plan: Prepare and disseminate an evacuation plan in case of fire or other emergency.
Emergency Contact: Have a visible and accessible list of emergency contacts.

5. Evaluation and Review

Conduct periodic assessments of occupational risks and update prevention measures as necessary.
Encourage employee participation in identifying risks and improving working conditions.

6. Conclusion

The safety and health of GillGalaxy employees is a priority. By implementing these occupational risk prevention measures, We are committed to maintaining a safe and healthy work environment.
