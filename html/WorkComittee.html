Occupational Risk Prevention Document

1. Introduction

Works council Committee Election ● Composition: For a company with 50 employees, the works council It will be composed of 5 members, elected by vote among all the workers.
● Election Process:
● Call: Elections are called, notifying all the employees with sufficient notice.
● Candidacies: Workers interested in being representatives can present their candidatures.
● Voting: All employees have the right to vote in a secret and direct election.
● Results: The candidates with the highest number of votes are elected as members of the committee.
Functions of the Works Committee
1. Collective Bargaining: Participate in the negotiation of conditions labor and salary with the management of the company.
2. Consultation and Participation: Acts as a consultative body in decisions important that affect workers, such as restructuring, schedule changes and health and safety policies.
3. Defense of Rights: Represents employees in case of conflicts labor and ensures compliance with labor laws.
4. Occupational Risk Assessment: Collaborate with the company in the identification of risks and the implementation of preventive measures.
5. Information and Transparency: Inform workers about the company decisions and ongoing negotiations.
6. Periodic Meetings: Maintains regular meetings with management to discuss work issues and find solutions to problems existing.
Rights of the Members of the Works Council
05/21/2024 ● Information: They have the right to receive all the information necessary to perform their functions.
● Time for Union Activities: They have a credit of hours paid monthly to dedicate to their representation activities.
● Legal Protection: They cannot be fired or sanctioned for reasons related to the performance of their representative functions. Workers' Rights Participation and Consultation
● Active Participation: Employees can actively participate in the election of their representatives.
● Direct Consultation: You can go to the works committee to express your concerns and suggestions.
Protection and Security
● Job Security: The works council collaborates to guarantee a safe and healthy work environment.
● Legal Advice: Provides information and advice on labor rights and duties.
