WORK WITH US

MY JOB

At Grill Galaxy, we are passionate about offering you a unique culinary experience. Our burgers are made with the freshest and highest quality ingredients. Each bite is designed to take you on a flavor journey that goes beyond the ordinary.

MY PASSION

Working at Grill Galaxy fits into your life and allows you to balance your daily activities. Whether you're studying, preparing for an exam, or have a hobby you're passionate about, we understand your personal needs and offer the flexibility you need. Our schedules are as varied as our people: you can choose to work only in the afternoons, only on weekends, or full-time. At Grill Galaxy, we believe in the importance of balancing work with your interests and personal commitments. Come and discover how we can work together to help you achieve your goals, both inside and outside of work.

MY PEOPLE

We don't say it, our crew does: "the best thing about the restaurant is the great atmosphere and the people. " At Grill Galaxy, we are committed to diversity, inclusion, and authenticity, and we integrate these values into our policies and working methods. That's why Grill Galaxy is a safe place where, no matter who you are, you'll find your place.
