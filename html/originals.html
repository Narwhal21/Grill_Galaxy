High quality meat

Angus beef is considered one of the highest quality meats on the market. This is because Angus animals are pasture-raised and provided with a balanced diet of grass and grains. Additionally, they are given enough time to mature, resulting in better quality meat.

What makes your meat different from the rest?

If you have not yet found the time to try the meat of this breed, you have to know that it is one of the highest quality meats that you can find on the market. Its main difference and distinctive feature is its intense and exquisite flavor, in addition to its juiciness and its excellent degree of marbling. The latter is due to the distribution of the fat that the animal generates within the muscle fibers, generating juicier, more flavorful meat.

ANGUS GRILL

Angus beef is known for being leaner than other beef varieties. This means it has less intramuscular fat, making it a healthier option. Additionally, it is an excellent option for those looking to lose weight or maintain a low-fat diet.
